All about me!

My name is Brandon Johnson. I am 23 years old and I am not from the Seattle area.
I am originally from Florida I grew up there and spent about 16 years of my life there.
I've lived in Washington for about 6 or 7 years? Not to sure on that one. But I grew to love the city and the people very fast.
Shortly after moving here I discovered my love of technology and computer science.
Seeing all the tech giants in one place was very inspiring, I wanted to be apart of that lifestyle.
With this new found inspiration I started looking for learning opertunities everywhere.
One of the first things was taking apart the home computer and breaking things all over the house.
That soon lead me on the path of software and coding. I started out small by modding games.
but soon went into writing small bits of code and little scripts for my favorite game series Fallout.
This was pretty fun but I always wanted more it just wasnt enough. I went out looking for new oppurtunities and found someone.
I was lucky enough to be introduced to one of the IT workers at my Mothers's company and he took me under his wing and started to mentor me.
With this new person mentoring me I was able to open up my scope of possibilites. I started learning to code small bits here and there.
I started with C++ and quickly moved onto python. I skipped around alot and looking back I wish I wouldnt have.
This was a recurring theme all through highschool and it only really stopped after I started my first year of college.
Once college started I was convinced that I wanted to be a game designer. But that was not the right choice.
Game design is fun in theory but not so much in practice at least for me. Moving on from that took a few years.
Eventually I realised that a more general less specialized background in code was right for me.
With some more practice on the side and taking care of some finacial issues I started looking and asking in my network about schools.
I was told about CodeFellows and now Im here! Im ready to start a new chapter in my life and Im excited to see it through.
